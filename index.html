How to Use It

A 10 minute daily habit
that compounds over time.

01 Morning Open the app. Read your Playbook rules before the market opens. Two minutes.
02 After Session Create a new entry. Log your P&L, upload screenshots, answer the three questions. Do this within one hour of closing your trades.
03 Weekly Review Filter your entries. Look for patterns. Adjust your Playbook based on what you find.
